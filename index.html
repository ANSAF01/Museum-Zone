About Us

Welcome to Museum Zone General Trading L.L.C.! We are your ultimate destination for all things fashion, offering a curated collection of exquisite fashion accessories since our establishment in 2010. Specializing in watches, perfumes, bags, wallets, sunglasses, and electronics, we pride ourselves on sourcing directly from trusted distributors such as Al Futtaim, Rivoli, Ahmed Seddiqi and Sons, ILG Group, Paris Gallery Perfumes, and esteemed Arabic Perfume Companies in UAE and beyond.

Our Expertise and Experience
With years of experience in the fashion industry, we have established ourselves as a reputable retailer known for offering the latest trends and top-notch quality products. Our focus on watches sets us apart, as we provide both wholesale and retail options for renowned fashion brands, ensuring that our customers have access to a wide selection of stylish timepieces.

Our Commitment to Quality
At Museum Zone General Trading L.L.C., we prioritize quality above all else. By sourcing our products directly from trusted distributors and fashion brand handling companies, we guarantee that each item in our collection meets the highest standards. Whether you are looking for a statement watch to elevate your outfit or a signature scent to complete your look, we have you covered with our carefully curated selection.

Our Mission and Vision
As a customer-centric company, our mission is to provide fashion enthusiasts with a seamless shopping experience. We strive to offer a diverse range of products that cater to different styles and preferences, ensuring that each customer finds the perfect piece that reflects their individuality. Our vision is to become the go-to destination for fashion lovers seeking quality, style, and affordability.
